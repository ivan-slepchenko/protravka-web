How to Use AviMate

Welcome to AviMate! Below are some important instructions to help you make the most of your experience with our AI co-pilot:

X-Plane Integration

If you're using X-Plane, make sure to install the ExtPlane plugin from here.

On-Screen Placement

AviMate should remain on the same screen as your simulator. Please note that if you're using multiple desktops, you won't be able to move AviMate to another desktop.

Simulator Connectivity

AviMate can establish a connection with your simulator only when you're in the world, not in the lobby.

Word Usage

- Your subscription plan determines the monthly limit of words you can use.
- AviMate counts the words you speak to it and the words it uses in response. Once you've reached your word limit for the month, AviMate will cease responding until the next month.

Features

Weather Reports:  You can ask for weather at any location using free form, but AviMate understands better if you mention an airport or town as a reference.
‍
Checklist Assistance: Use free form to start a checklist and mention the procedure. Not all planes are supported yet, so AviMate may provide an average checklist.

Currently supported checklists include:
- Airbus A318
- Airbus A300/A310
- Airbus A319/A320
- Boeing 737/737 NG/MAX
- Boeing 747/757/767
- Boeing 787
- Bombardier CRJ/Dash 8/Q
- Cessna Citation 700/152/172
- Embraer E175/E195
- Airbus H135/H145/H165
- Asobo NXCub

Points of Interests: During flight, ask AviMate for points of interest nearby and request guidance to them. Feel free to ask questions about points of interest in free form.

Aviation Trivia: AviMate knows trivia about locations you fly over. Ask in free form, and be surprised by the fascinating facts.
‍
Airport Frequencies: Ask AviMate for airport frequencies in free form. If you're using Vatsim, inquire about the active frequency at any airport or location.

Discovery Flight: Embark on a discovery flight like never before. Let AviMate guide you through locations and share interesting insights about the places you see.
‍
Aviation History and Education: AviMate is well-versed in aviation history, theory, airspaces, systems, and terminology. Educate yourself by asking questions and exploring the vast knowledge base.

Response Time

Currently, the initial response from AviMate may take up to 5 seconds. Our team is actively working to reduce this latency
